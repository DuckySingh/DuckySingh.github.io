About Us

We are Mr. Ducky and Miss Singha. Welcome to our world!

A passionate runner from the fiery sign of Aries. She is what one calls a true martian. Anger sits on her nose and distress sings its calls whenever the slightest of trouble occurs. Don 't go with her angel like face or the bright smile that can change even the coldest winters to a new spring. She is like a new born baby, no prejudices with anything at all. But once she has made her mind to something she will do it no matter what!. And god forbid anything does not go exactly as she plans in her head. She'll start her infant cry.

Well you can't deny this either that her charm lies in that very thing. She's a queen and she knows it. She relishes her place. She's a cuckoo when she sings and is nothing short of a picasso if she wants to paint! Well if she wants that is xD.

Currently she is doing her undergrad from the famous Arya Vidyapeeth College, she is a specialist in zoology. But talk to her about plants, rare medicinal herbs, or maybe even trending movies and gossips. She can charm it all alike!
